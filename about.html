Founded in 2008

Luxe Scent was born from a single obsession: the belief that fragrance is not a luxury product but an art form. Founded in a small Paris atelier by master perfumer Elise Vernard, our house began with three fragrances and a commitment to using only the finest natural and ethically sourced ingredients.

Over seventeen years, we have grown into an internationally recognized fragrance house while remaining independent, refusing acquisition by the large conglomerates that dominate the industry. Every decision - from ingredient sourcing to bottle design - remains guided by artistic integrity rather than market trends.

Our Philosophy

We believe great fragrance should be the result of patience. Our perfumers spend years composing a single scent, allowing it to evolve, breathe, and find its own truth before it ever reaches a bottle. We reject the industry practice of fast creation. Some of our fragrances took a decade to complete.

Sustainability is woven into everything we do. We work directly with farmers and distillers, paying fair prices, investing in replanting programs, and exploring the finest ethical alternatives where wild harvest would threaten biodiversity.

Our Promise

Every bottle carries our personal guarantee: natural, ethically sourced ingredients wherever possible, no synthetic shortcuts that compromise the quality of the finished scent, transparent labeling of all materials, and a lifetime refill program so that your bottle never needs to be discarded.
